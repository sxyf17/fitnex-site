01 — Overview

Overview

Fitnex ("we", "our", or "us") is a fitness tracking application that helps you log workouts, track cardio, generate AI-powered training plans, and monitor your progress over time.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you use Fitnex, why we collect it, and how we use and protect it. By using Fitnex, you acknowledge the data practices described in this policy.

Short version: We only collect data that makes Fitnex work well for you. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
02 — Controller & Scope

Data Controller & Scope

For the purposes of applicable data protection laws, Fitnex is the data controller for personal information processed through the app.

This policy applies to information collected through the mobile app, related backend services, and support communications. It does not apply to third-party websites or services that have their own privacy policies.

03 — What We Collect

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account Information

  • Email address (used to create and identify your account)
  • Display name or username (if provided)
  • Authentication data (managed securely via Firebase Authentication)

Fitness & Health Data

  • Workout logs — exercises, sets, reps, weights, and duration
  • Cardio activity data — type, distance, duration, and calories burned
  • Body metrics — height, weight, and fitness goals (if provided)
  • AI-generated workout preferences and history

Device & Usage Data

  • Device identifiers (for analytics and crash reporting)
  • App usage patterns and feature interactions (aggregated and anonymized)
  • Crash logs and performance data to improve stability

Location Data

  • Approximate location may be collected during cardio tracking sessions (e.g. outdoor runs) if you grant location permission
  • Location access is optional and can be disabled at any time in your device settings
04 — How We Use It

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Fitnex app and its features
  • Personalize AI-generated workout plans based on your profile and history
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Track your fitness progress and display it within the app
  • Send important account notifications (e.g. service and subscription notices)
  • Analyze usage trends to fix bugs and improve the user experience
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and comply with legal obligations
AI Features: When you use AI generation features, relevant profile and prompt data is sent to our backend and processed using Google Generative AI (Gemini) to create workout results.
05 — Legal Bases

Legal Bases for Processing

If you are in a jurisdiction that requires a legal basis for processing (such as the EEA/UK), we process personal data under one or more of the following bases:

  • Performance of a contract: to provide app functionality you request (account, workout tracking, subscriptions)
  • Legitimate interests: to improve security, reliability, and product performance
  • Consent: for optional permissions (such as location or tracking where required)
  • Legal obligations: where required to comply with applicable law
06 — Data Sharing

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. We may share your information only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and cloud functions. These providers process data on our behalf under strict data protection agreements.
  • AI processing: AI prompts and related context are processed via Google Generative AI through our backend to deliver AI features.
  • Analytics: Aggregated or de-identified usage data may be used to understand app performance and usage patterns.
  • Legal obligations: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Fitnex and its users.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified of any such change.
07 — Storage, Security & Retention

Data Storage, Security & Retention

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure, which includes:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • Secure authentication tokens managed by Firebase Auth
  • Access controls that restrict data to your account only

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, including legal, accounting, and security obligations. If you delete your account, we aim to delete or de-identify associated personal data from active systems within 30 days, subject to limited retention required by law or technical backup cycles.

08 — International Transfers

International Data Transfers

Because Fitnex uses cloud providers with global infrastructure, your information may be processed outside your country of residence.

Where required by law, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers, such as contractual protections and reliance on provider compliance frameworks.

09 — Your Rights

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Portability: Request a portable copy of certain data where applicable.
  • Objection/Restriction: Object to or request restriction of certain processing where legally available.
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for optional processing at any time.
  • Location permissions: Disable location access at any time via your device settings without affecting core app functionality.
  • Opt out of non-essential processing: Contact us for available opt-out options.

To exercise your rights, contact us at dev.saif17@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

10 — US State Disclosures

US State Privacy Disclosures

If you are a resident of California or another US state with privacy laws, you may have additional rights, such as the right to know, delete, correct, and opt out of certain data uses.

Fitnex does not sell personal information for money. We also do not knowingly process sensitive personal information for inferring characteristics beyond app functionality.

You may exercise applicable rights by contacting us at dev.saif17@gmail.com. You may designate an authorized agent where allowed by law.

11 — Tracking, Ads & Permissions

Tracking, Ads & Device Permissions

  • Ads: Free-tier users may receive ads through Google AdMob.
  • Tracking permissions: On platforms that require consent (such as iOS App Tracking Transparency), we request consent before enabling tracking-dependent ad features.
  • Do Not Track: Because mobile apps do not consistently support browser-based DNT signals, we do not respond to DNT in a standardized way.
  • Permissions: You can control app permissions (such as location and notifications) in your device settings.
12 — Automated Processing

Automated Processing (AI)

Fitnex uses automated systems to generate workout suggestions. These outputs are informational and may not be accurate for every user or health condition.

Fitnex does not make solely automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects about users.

13 — Children's Privacy

Children's Privacy

Fitnex is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at dev.saif17@gmail.com.

14 — Third-Party Services

Third-Party Services

Fitnex integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Firestore database, and Cloud Functions — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob: Advertising shown to free-tier users — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Generative AI (Gemini): AI content generation via our backend — ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms
  • RevenueCat: Subscription status and entitlement management — revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store and Google Play: Marketplace billing and subscription handling — apple.com/legal/privacy / policies.google.com/privacy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

15 — Security Incidents

Security Incidents

If we become aware of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will take steps required by applicable law, including investigation, mitigation, and notice where legally required.

16 — Changes

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you via the app or by email.

Your continued use of Fitnex after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
