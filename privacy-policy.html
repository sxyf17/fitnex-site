01 — Overview

Overview

Fitnex ("we", "our", or "us") is a fitness tracking application that helps you log workouts, track cardio, generate AI-powered training plans, and monitor your progress over time.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you use Fitnex, why we collect it, and how we use and protect it. By using Fitnex, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Short version: We only collect data that makes Fitnex work well for you. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
02 — What We Collect

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account Information

  • Email address (used to create and identify your account)
  • Display name or username (if provided)
  • Authentication data (managed securely via Firebase Authentication)

Fitness & Health Data

  • Workout logs — exercises, sets, reps, weights, and duration
  • Cardio activity data — type, distance, duration, and calories burned
  • Body metrics — height, weight, and fitness goals (if provided)
  • AI-generated workout preferences and history

Device & Usage Data

  • Device identifiers (for analytics and crash reporting)
  • App usage patterns and feature interactions (aggregated and anonymized)
  • Crash logs and performance data to improve stability

Location Data

  • Approximate location may be collected during cardio tracking sessions (e.g. outdoor runs) if you grant location permission
  • Location access is optional and can be disabled at any time in your device settings
03 — How We Use It

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Fitnex app and its features
  • Personalize AI-generated workout plans based on your profile and history
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Track your fitness progress and display it within the app
  • Send important account notifications (e.g. subscription confirmations)
  • Analyze usage trends to fix bugs and improve the user experience
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and comply with legal obligations
AI Features: When you use the AI Workout Generator, your profile data (goals, experience level, equipment) is sent to our backend to generate a personalized plan. This data is not stored by the AI model and is used solely to produce your workout.
04 — Data Sharing

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. We may share your information only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and cloud functions. These providers process data on our behalf under strict data protection agreements.
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage data may be shared with analytics providers to help us understand how the app is used.
  • Legal obligations: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Fitnex and its users.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified of any such change.
05 — Storage & Security

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure, which includes:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • Secure authentication tokens managed by Firebase Auth
  • Access controls that restrict data to your account only

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed from our systems within 30 days.

06 — Your Rights

Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Location permissions: Disable location access at any time via your device settings without affecting core app functionality.
  • Opt out of analytics: Contact us to opt out of non-essential data collection.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at dev.saif17@gmail.com.

07 — Children's Privacy

Children's Privacy

Fitnex is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at dev.saif17@gmail.com.
